GOOGLE FONTS

Google Fonts is a vast library of free and open-source web fonts that allows website developers and designers to easily access and use a wide variety of fonts in their projects. It was launched by Google in 2010 with the goal of improving typography on the web and making it easier for developers to choose and implement high-quality fonts. Google Fonts offers a valuable resource for web designers and developers, providing a wide range of high-quality fonts that are free to use, easy to integrate, and optimized for performance and cross-browser compatibility. Whether you're working on a personal blog, a professional website, or a web application, Google Fonts provides an extensive selection of fonts to enhance the typography and visual appeal of your projects.
